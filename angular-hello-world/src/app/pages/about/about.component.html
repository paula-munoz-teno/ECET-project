ECET Information

What is ECET?

A research project from the Universidad Autónoma de Madrid (UAM) that is developing a teaching support tool (the ECET) to efficiently create high-quality multiple-choice exams.

Why Does It Emerge?

  • Because there are no methods to evaluate the quality of exams before they are applied.
  • Because, in general, teachers have not received training to construct exams.
  • Because the exam construction process is not systematized or regulated.
  • To improve the conditions of the university evaluation system.

Why Must It Continue?

Due to the evidence of reliability and validity of the ECET obtained in the pilot study:

  • ICC: 0.94
  • α: 0.74
  • R²: 0.64
  • Inter-rater reliability
  • Internal consistency
  • Predictive validity of ECET f(α)
